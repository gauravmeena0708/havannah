Havannah Game

Player 1 is by default Human(mostly you). You can choose between Human, AI(medium level), or AI2(bit hard) for Player 2.

How to play:
Each player has to try to form bridge (connecting two corners), fork (connecting 3 edges) or ring as given in image above while blocking opponent.
